Privacy Policy — Awon

Operated by The Rival Is Me (therivalisme.com) · Effective: July 16, 2026 · Contact: info@therivalisme.com

Awon is a content and store operations application for The Rival Is Me. It connects to TikTok through TikTok's official APIs (Login Kit and Content Posting API) to help the connected account owner prepare and publish their own original video content. This policy explains what data Awon accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected.

What data we access

  • TikTok account data: when the account owner connects TikTok via OAuth, Awon receives an access token and, through TikTok's APIs, basic creator information (display name, username, avatar, posting options such as available privacy levels and interaction settings) and the list and performance statistics of videos on the connected account.
  • Content the account owner provides: raw video footage uploaded by the account owner for editing, and the edited videos, captions, and hashtags prepared from that footage.
  • Awon does not access TikTok direct messages, contacts, or data about any TikTok user other than the connected account.

How we use it

  • To display accurate posting options (privacy levels, interaction settings) before any post is made.
  • To publish videos to the connected TikTok account — only content originating from the account owner's own uploaded footage, and only with the account owner's authorization.
  • To show the account owner performance statistics of their own published videos.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share TikTok data with any third party.
  • We do not use TikTok data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than operating the connected account.
  • We do not post content without authorization from the connected account owner.
  • We do not collect data about minors. The service is operated by and for adults.

Storage and security

  • Access tokens and content are stored on a private, access-controlled server (Railway) and are not exposed publicly.
  • API credentials and secrets are stored as environment variables, never in source code or public repositories.
  • Uploaded footage and edited clips are retained only as long as needed for content production; edited clips are deleted after posting or discarding.

Data retention and deletion

The connected account owner can disconnect TikTok at any time by revoking the app's access in TikTok's settings (Settings & privacy → Security & permissions → Apps and services), which invalidates the stored token. Uploaded footage and derived content can be deleted at any time on request to info@therivalisme.com; requests are honored within 30 days.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
